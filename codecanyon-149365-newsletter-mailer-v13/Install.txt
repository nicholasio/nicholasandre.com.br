Install
------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) Create a mysql database for the "Newsletter Mailer" on your web server, as well as a MySQL user who has all privileges for accessing and modifying it.

2) Place the Newsletter Mailer files in the desired location on your web server.

3) Make sure that you have uploaded all the files there are three .htaccess files wiche are hidden on Mac and Linux. The First .htaccess is under the newsletter folder, the second under the subfolder app and the last one is under the subfolder app/webroot

4) Make the tmp and conf folder inclusive subfolders writeable.

5) Visit the Newsletter Mailer in your Browser.
